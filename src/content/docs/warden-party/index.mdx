## Warden Party

**Warden Party** is a configurable Minecraft boss plugin built around custom Warden encounters, scheduled spawns, natural Warden replacement, reward commands, and a Phantom-based vulnerability phase.

It is designed for servers that want a repeatable world boss experience without hard-coding each event by hand. You define boss variants, configure when and where they appear, control how players can damage them, and automate rewards for participation and top performers.
