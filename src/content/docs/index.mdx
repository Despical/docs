<CardGrid>
    <LinkCard title="Easter Eggs" href="/easter-eggs" icon="easter-eggs">
        **Easter Eggs** is a Minecraft plugin that lets you add interactive, feature-rich **Easter Eggs** to your server, bringing more fun and engagement to gameplay.
    </LinkCard>

    <LinkCard title="Santa Says" href="/santa-says" icon="santa-says" tone="red">
        **Santa Says** is a fast-paced Minecraft minigame where players complete quick, interactive tasks to earn points.
        Featuring a mix of participation and placement-based challenges, festive task variations, and competitive leaderboards,
    </LinkCard>

    <LinkCard title="Advanced Parkour" href="/advanced-parkour" icon="advanced-parkour" tone="teal">
        **Advanced Parkour** is an arena-based Minecraft parkour plugin with checkpoint routing, personal best tracking,
        leaderboards, music support, setup menus, and deep per-arena customization.
    </LinkCard>

    <LinkCard title="Eat Everything" href="/eat-everything" icon="eat-everything" tone="copper">
        **Eat Everything** is a Paper plugin focused on modern food components, letting you turn any item into a configurable consumable
        with custom nutrition, saturation, effects, leftovers, and reusable YAML-based food presets.
    </LinkCard>

    <LinkCard title="Carousel" href="/carousel" icon="carousel" tone="orange">
        **Carousel** is a feature-rich Minecraft plugin that brings beautifully animated, fully rideable carousels to your server, creating an immersive and
        memorable attraction for your players.
    </LinkCard>

    <LinkCard title="Command Framework" href="/command-framework" icon="command-framework" tone="amber">
        **Command Framework** is a lightweight, annotation-based command system designed to reduce boilerplate code, eliminate the manual command registration,
        and make your commands cleaner and easier to manage.
    </LinkCard>

    <LinkCard title="Warden Party" href="/warden-party" icon="warden-party" tone="blue">
        **Warden Party** transforms your server into a battleground with intense Warden boss fights. With fully customizable mechanics, timed events, natural spawns, deadly Phantom phases, and automated rewards, it delivers unforgettable server-wide boss fights that players will compete to conquer.
    </LinkCard>
</CardGrid>
