## Carousel 🎠

**Carousel** is a unique Minecraft plugin that lets you create fully rideable, interactive carousels to bring the fairground experience to your server.
Whether you want a giant, slow-moving ferris wheel or a small, fast-spinning top, Carousel gives you the tools to build and customize it all in real-time.

Designed for performance and ease of use, it's the perfect addition to server hubs, theme parks, and survival spawns.
